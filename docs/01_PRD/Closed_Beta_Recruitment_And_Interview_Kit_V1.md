# ZEROON Closed-Beta Recruitment and Interview Kit V1

Status: Ready for operator review; invitations remain gated
Date: 2026-07-23

## Recruitment promise

ZEROON is looking for at most 20 adults who want a quiet, private way to keep
and revisit moments over time. Recruitment is for reflective continuity, not
therapy, crisis support, romance, productivity scoring, plush interest, or AI
novelty.

Use this invitation copy:

> ZEROON is a private long-term companion and memory space. In this closed
> Beta, you can leave brief Records, revisit your Archive, control what ZEROON
> remembers, and decide whether any content-free product evidence is collected.
> The Beta is for adults and may contain unfinished behavior. If you are
> interested in using it naturally for two weeks, reply to the ZEROON team.

Do not promise continuous support, diagnosis, guaranteed availability, public
release timing, or a reward that has not been separately approved.

## Screening

An operator confirms only:

- the participant is 18 or older; the target learning cohort is age 25-45;
- they expect to use ZEROON for ordinary private reflection and continuity;
- they understand ZEROON is not emergency, medical, legal, financial, or
  psychological care;
- they can use Simplified Chinese or English;
- they can contact `zeroon_ai@outlook.com` if access or safety boundaries fail;
- they are willing to use the product naturally for about two weeks.

Do not ask for diagnosis, therapy history, crisis history, intimate content,
income, exact employer, or Record/Memory samples during screening.

## In-product notice versus interview consent

The in-product Beta notice covers adult eligibility and optional content-free
product evidence only. It does not grant interview consent.

Before any interview, send this separate consent:

> We would like to ask about your experience using ZEROON. Participation is
> optional. You may skip any question or stop at any time. We will take
> product-research notes about what worked, what felt unclear, and what you
> expected. Please do not share private Record, Memory, conversation, password,
> verification code, or other content you do not want in research notes. We
> will not record audio or video unless we ask separately and you explicitly
> agree. Declining or stopping the interview does not affect your ZEROON
> account.

Record interview consent as `accepted`, `declined`, or `withdrawn` in the
research log. Do not store it as a product evidence event.

## Interview guide

1. What did you think ZEROON was for before your first use?
2. Which moment made its long-term value clearest, if any?
3. Did saving and later revisiting a Record feel connected?
4. Did Memory and AI-use controls match what you expected?
5. Was anything too much like a diary, task tool, or generic AI chat?
6. Was any privacy or support boundary unclear?
7. What would make you return next week without reminders or pressure?
8. What should ZEROON never become?

Avoid asking participants to read private Records or Memory aloud. Ask about
the interaction and expectation, not the private content.

## Synthesis template

For each interview, record:

- participant research code, not mobile number or evidence subject id;
- interview date and consent state;
- expected product promise;
- activation or continuity friction described in the participant's own general
  terms;
- trust/control issue;
- support/reliability issue;
- one product-mainline signal;
- one drift warning;
- follow-up decision.

Across interviews, synthesize themes only after at least five participants
contribute to a theme. Do not create personality labels, emotional scores, or
individual behavior rankings.
